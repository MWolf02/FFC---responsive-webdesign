Bob Proctor

The man who changed millions of peoples lives

Here are some of the things he accomplished:

  • 1956 - Started working with Earl Nightingale, a motivational speaker.
  • 1961 - Established his own company, Bob Proctor Consultants.
  • 1973 - Published his first book, "You Were Born Rich."
  • 1984 - Featured in the self-help film "The Secret."
  • 1997 - Founded the Proctor Gallagher Institute with Sandy Gallagher.
  • 2006 - Released the program "Six Minutes to Success."
  • 2010 - Became a part of the Napoleon Hill Foundation Board of Directors.
  • 2016 - Celebrated his 82nd birthday and continued active coaching.
  • 2019 - Launched the "Paradigm Shift" seminar series.
  • 2021 - Continued to inspire millions through online platforms and events.

"You are the only problem you will ever have and you are the only solution. Change is inevitable; personal growth is always a personal decision."
